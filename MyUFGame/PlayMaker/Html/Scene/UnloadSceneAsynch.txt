<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Unload a scene asynchronously by its name or index in Build Settings. Destroys all GameObjects associated with the given scene and removes the scene from the SceneManager.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene Reference</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The reference options of the Scene</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene By Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the scene to load. The given sceneName can either be the last part of the path, without .unity extension or the full path still without the .unity extension</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene At Build Index</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The build index of the scene to unload.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene At Index</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The index of the scene to unload.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene By Path</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The scene Path.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene By Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject unload scene of</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Operation Priority</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
lets you tweak in which order async operation calls will be performed. Leave to none for default</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Progress</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The loading's progress.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Is Done</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
True when loading is done</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Done Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event sent when scene loading is done</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scene Not Found Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event sent if the scene to load was not found</div>
</div>
