<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Interpolates between from and to by t and normalizes the result afterwards.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
From Quaternion</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
From Quaternion.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
To Quaternion</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
To Quaternion.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Amount</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate between fromQuaternion and toQuaternion by this amount. Value is clamped to 0-1 range. 0 = fromQuaternion; 1 = toQuaternion; 0.5 = half way between.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Result</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the result in this quaternion variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if any of the values are changing.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame Option</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Defines how to perform the action when 'every Frame' is enabled.</div>
</div>
