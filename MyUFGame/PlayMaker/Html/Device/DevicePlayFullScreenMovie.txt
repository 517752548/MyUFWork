<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Plays a full-screen movie on a handheld device. Please consult the Unity docs for Handheld.PlayFullScreenMovie for proper usage.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Movie Path</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Note that player will stream movie directly from the iPhone disc, therefore you have to provide movie as a separate files and not as an usual asset.
You will have to create a folder named StreamingAssets inside your Unity project (inside your Assets folder). Store your movies inside that folder. Unity will automatically copy contents of that folder into the iPhone application bundle.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Fade Color</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
This action will initiate a transition that fades the screen from your current content to the designated background color of the player. When playback finishes, the player uses another fade effect to transition back to your content.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Movie Control Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Options for displaying movie playback controls. See Unity docs.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Movie Scaling Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Scaling modes for displaying movies.. See Unity docs.</div>
</div>
