<p>
Moves a Game Object with a Character Controller. Velocity along the y-axis is ignored. Speed is in meters/s. Gravity is automatically applied.</p>
</div>

	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>

The GameObject to move.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Move Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The movement vector.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Multiply the movement vector by a speed factor.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Space</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Move in local or world space.</div>
</div>
