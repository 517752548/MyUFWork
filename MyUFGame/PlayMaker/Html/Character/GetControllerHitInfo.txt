<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Gets info on the last Character Controller collision and store in variables.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object Hit</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the GameObject hit in the last collision.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Contact Point</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the contact point of the last collision in world coordinates.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Contact Normal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the normal of the last collision.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Move Direction</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the direction of the last move before the collision.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Move Length</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the distance of the last move before the collision.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Physics Material Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the physics material of the Game Object Hit. Useful for triggering different effects. Audio, particles...</div>
</div>
