<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Adds a 2d force to a Game Object. Use Vector2 variable and/or Float variables for each axis.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject to apply the force to.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Force Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Option for applying the force</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
At Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Optionally apply the force at a position on the object. This will also add some torque. The position is often returned from MousePick or GetCollision2dInfo actions.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A Vector2 force to add. Optionally override any axis with the X, Y parameters.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
X</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Force along the X axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Y</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Force along the Y axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector 3</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A Vector3 force to add. z is ignored</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
