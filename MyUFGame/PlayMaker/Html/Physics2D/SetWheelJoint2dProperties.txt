<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Sets the various properties of a WheelJoint2d component</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The WheelJoint2d target</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Use Motor</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Should a motor force be applied automatically to the Rigidbody2D?</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Motor Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The desired speed for the Rigidbody2D to reach as it moves with the joint.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Max Motor Torque</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The maximum force that can be applied to the Rigidbody2D at the joint to attain the target speed.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Angle</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The world angle along which the suspension will move. This provides 2D constrained motion similar to a SliderJoint2D. This is typically how suspension works in the real world.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Damping Ratio</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The amount by which the suspension spring force is reduced in proportion to the movement speed.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Frequency</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The frequency at which the suspension spring oscillates.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
