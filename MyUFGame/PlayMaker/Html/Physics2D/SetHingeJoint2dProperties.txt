<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Sets the various properties of a HingeJoint2d component</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The HingeJoint2d target</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Use Limits</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Should limits be placed on the range of rotation?</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Min</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Lower angular limit of rotation.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Max</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Upper angular limit of rotation</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Use Motor</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Should a motor force be applied automatically to the Rigidbody2D?</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Motor Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The desired speed for the Rigidbody2D to reach as it moves with the joint.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Max Motor Torque</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The maximum force that can be applied to the Rigidbody2D at the joint to attain the target speed.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
