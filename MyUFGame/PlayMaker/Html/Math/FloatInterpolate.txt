</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolation mode: Linear or EaseInOut.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
From Float</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate from this value.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
To Float</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate to this value.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Time</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate over this amount of time in seconds.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Result</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the current value in a float variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Finish Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send when the interpolation is finished.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">

	<div id="paramDesc">
