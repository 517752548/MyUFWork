<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Sets the position, rotation and weights of an IK goal. A GameObject can be set to control the position and rotation, or it can be manually expressed.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The target.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
IK Goal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The IK goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Goal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The gameObject target of the ik goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The position of the ik goal. If Goal GameObject set, position is used as an offset from Goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Rotation</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The rotation of the ik goal.If Goal GameObject set, rotation is used as an offset from Goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Position Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The translative weight of an IK goal (0 = at the original animation before IK, 1 = at the goal)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Rotation Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Sets the rotational weight of an IK goal (0 = rotation before IK, 1 = rotation at the IK goal)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful when changing over time.</div>
</div>
