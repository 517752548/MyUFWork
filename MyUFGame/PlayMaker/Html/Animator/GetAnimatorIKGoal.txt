<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Gets the position, rotation and weights of an IK goal. A GameObject can be set to use for the position and rotation</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The target. An Animator component is required</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
IK Goal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The IK goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Goal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The gameObject to apply ik goal position and rotation to</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Gets The position of the ik goal. If Goal GameObject define, position is used as an offset from Goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Rotation</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Gets The rotation of the ik goal.If Goal GameObject define, rotation is used as an offset from Goal</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Position Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Gets The translative weight of an IK goal (0 = at the original animation before IK, 1 = at the goal)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Rotation Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Gets the rotational weight of an IK goal (0 = rotation before IK, 1 = rotation at the IK goal)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame Option</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Select when to perform the action, during OnUpdate, OnAnimatorMove, OnAnimatorIK</div>
</div>
