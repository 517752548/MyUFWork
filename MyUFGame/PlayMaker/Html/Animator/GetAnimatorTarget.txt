<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Gets the position and rotation of the target specified by SetTarget(AvatarTarget targetIndex, float targetNormalizedTime)).
The position and rotation are only valid when a frame has being evaluated after the SetTarget call</p>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">

	<div id="paramDesc">
The target rotation</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
