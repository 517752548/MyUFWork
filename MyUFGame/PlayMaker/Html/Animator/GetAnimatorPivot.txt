<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Returns the pivot weight and/or position. The pivot is the most stable point between the avatar's left and right foot.
 For a weight value of 0, the left foot is the most stable point For a value of 1, the right foot is the most stable point</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The target. An Animator component is required</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Pivot Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The pivot is the most stable point between the avatar's left and right foot.
 For a value of 0, the left foot is the most stable point For a value of 1, the right foot is the most stable point</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Pivot Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The pivot is the most stable point between the avatar's left and right foot.
 For a value of 0, the left foot is the most stable point For a value of 1, the right foot is the most stable point</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame.</div>
</div>
