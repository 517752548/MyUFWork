<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Plays a state. This could be used to synchronize your animation with audio or synchronize an Animator over the network.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The target. An Animator component is required</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
State Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the state that will be played.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Layer</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The layer where the state is.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Normalized Time</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The normalized time at which the state will play</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful when using normalizedTime to manually control the animation.</div>
</div>
