<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Sends Events based on the comparison of 2 Colors.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Color 1</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The first Color.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Color 2</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The second Color.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Tolerance</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Tolerance of test, to test for 'almost equals' or to ignore small floating point rounding differences.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Equal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event sent if Color 1 equals Color 2 (within Tolerance)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Not Equal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event sent if Color 1 does not equal Color 2 (within Tolerance)</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if the variables are changing and you're waiting for a particular result.</div>
</div>
