<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Captures the current pose of a hierarchy as an animation clip.

Useful to blend from an arbitrary pose (e.g. a rag-doll death) back to a known animation (e.g. idle).</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject root of the hierarchy to capture.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Capture position keys.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Rotation</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Capture rotation keys.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Scale</div>

</div>
