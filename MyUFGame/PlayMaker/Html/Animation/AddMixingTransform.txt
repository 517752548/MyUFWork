<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Play an animation on a subset of the hierarchy. E.g., A waving animation on the upper body.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject playing the animation.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Animation Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the animation to mix. NOTE: The animation should already be added to the Animation Component on the GameObject.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Transform</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The mixing transform. E.g., root/upper_body/left_shoulder</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Recursive</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
If recursive is true all children of the mix transform will also be animated.</div>
</div>
