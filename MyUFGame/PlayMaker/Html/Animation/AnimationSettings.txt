<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Set the Wrap Mode, Blend Mode, Layer and Speed of an Animation.
NOTE: Settings are applied once, on entering the state, NOT continuously. To dynamically control an animation's settings, use Set Animation Speed etc.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A GameObject with an Animation Component.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Anim Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the animation.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Wrap Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The behavior of the animation when it wraps.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Blend Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
How the animation is blended with other animations on the Game Object.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The speed of the animation. 1 = normal; 2 = double speed...</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Layer</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The animation layer</div>
</div>
