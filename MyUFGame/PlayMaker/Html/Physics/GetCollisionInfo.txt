<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Gets info on the last collision event and store in variables. See Unity Physics docs.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object Hit</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the GameObject hit.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Relative Velocity</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the relative velocity of the collision.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Relative Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the relative speed of the collision. Useful for controlling reactions. E.g., selecting an appropriate sound fx.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Contact Point</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the world position of the collision contact. Useful for spawning effects etc.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Contact Normal</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the collision normal vector. Useful for aligning spawned effects etc.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Physics Material Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Get the name of the physics material of the colliding GameObject. Useful for triggering different effects. Audio, particles...</div>
</div>
