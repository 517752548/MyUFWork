<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Detect collisions between Game Objects that have RigidBody/Collider components.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject to detect collisions on.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Collision</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The type of collision to detect.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Collide Tag</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Filter by Tag.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Send Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send if a collision is detected.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Collider</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the GameObject that collided with the Owner of this FSM.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Force</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the force of the collision. NOTE: Use Get Collision Info to get more info about the collision.</div>
</div>
