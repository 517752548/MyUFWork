<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Adds torque (rotational force) to a Game Object.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject to add torque to.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A Vector3 torque. Optionally override any axis with the X, Y, Z parameters.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
X</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Torque around the X axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Y</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Torque around the Y axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Z</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Torque around the Z axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Space</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Apply the force in world or local space.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Force Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The type of force to apply. See Unity Physics docs.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
