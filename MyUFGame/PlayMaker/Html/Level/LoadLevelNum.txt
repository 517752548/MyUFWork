<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Loads a Level by Index number. Before you can load a level, you have to add it to the list of levels defined in File->Build Settings...</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Level Index</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The level index in File->Build Settings</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Additive</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Load the level additively, keeping the current scene.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Loaded Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send after the level is loaded.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Dont Destroy On Load</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Keep this GameObject in the new level. NOTE: The GameObject and components is disabled then enabled on load; uncheck Reset On Disable to keep the active state.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Failed Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send if the level cannot be loaded.</div>
</div>
