<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Moves a Vector2 towards a Target. Optionally sends an event when successful.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Source</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The Vector2 to Move</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Target</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A target Vector2 to move towards.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Max Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The maximum movement speed. HINT: You can make this a variable to change it over time.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Finish Distance</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Distance at which the move is considered finished, and the Finish Event is sent.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Finish Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send when the Finish Distance is reached.</div>
</div>
