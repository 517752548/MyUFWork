<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Samples a Color on a continuous Colors gradient.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Colors</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Array of colors to defining the gradient.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Sample At</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Point on the gradient to sample. Should be between 0 and the number of colors in the gradient.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Color</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the sampled color in a Color variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
